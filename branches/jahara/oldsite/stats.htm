Stat Generator for MUME (VIII)

Due to popular demand another stat column has been added enabeling people to compare two different sets of stats. This, together with the field Reroll Calc, makes it possible to see how you can change your stats after a Partial reroll.

Stat data can be entered in a couple ways:

  1. Enter your stats using the and until you get the desired number
  2. Enter your stats by directly entering the figure into the text box
  3. Pasting one's stats into the Stat Filter and following it's instructions

To get your base stats from your character in MUME type: info %B

As always you can save the stats of the column Stat Generator in the textarea below by pressing the button Add "Add to List " to list. This way you can continue with more simulations without forgetting the old ones.

Tip: Hovering your cursor over a certain skill will reveal it's required stats.
